# Mobile Crates

## Mixer w/ Digital Wireless Mics

This mobile cart offers a completely self-contained audio system which can provides digital wireless microphones and a 16 channel mixer which could be integrated with any house system. For instance, this could be integrated with the Lawrenceville Performing Arts Center (LPAC) commonly used for the regional GHSA One Act Play competitions.

## Mobile Analog Mic Case

This system primarily contains analog wireless microphones which should NOT be utilized within the house system due to overlap with identical receivers (SLX4 J3). These should be limited ONLY to use outside of the DHS theatre.

| QTY | Equipment | Notes |
|-----|-----------|-------|
| 1 | [Shure UA844+SWB](https://www.shure.com/en-US/products/accessories/ua844p?variant=UA844+SWB) | Active Antenna Distribution (470-960 MHz) |
| 2 | [Shure-Compatible UHF Antenna](https://a.co/d/cXZsKYc) | Active Directional Antennas (400-970 MHz) |
| 6 | [Shure SLX4 (J3 572–596 MHz)](https://www.shure.com/en-US/docs/guide/SLX)<sup>2</sup> | Analog Receivers for Belt Packs WMIC11-16 |
| 1 | Tascam CD-200 | CD Player |

> <sup>2</sup> Due to limited space within the 572-596 MHz space and overlap from local high-powered TV stations, the use of these receivers is NOT recommended within the DHS theatre. The chance of interference and drop-outs is high and is made worse due to overcrowding with similar SLX4 models in the tech booth.
