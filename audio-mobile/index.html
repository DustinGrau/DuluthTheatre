Mobile Crates

Mixer w/ Digital Wireless Mics

This mobile cart offers a completely self-contained audio system which can provides digital wireless microphones and a 16 channel mixer which could be integrated with any house system. For instance, this could be integrated with the Lawrenceville Performing Arts Center (LPAC) commonly used for the regional GHSA One Act Play competitions.

QTY Equipment Notes
1 Mackie 1604VLZ4 16 Ch. Analog Audio Mixer
2 Shure UA844+SWB Active Antenna Distribution (470-960 MHz)
2 Shure UA834WB Antennas In-Line Antenna Amplifier (470-900 MHz)
10 Shure QLXD4 (G50 470-534 MHz) Digital Receivers for Belt Packs WL1-10
2 Sennheiser EW 100 G3 (566-608 MHz)1 Wireless Mics (Lapel)
1 Furman M-8x2 Power Distribution

1 As of July 2020, frequencies above 608 MHz (specifically 617–652 MHz, 657–663 MHz, and 698–806 MHz) are illegal to operate due to overlap with cellular networks. Ensure that any chosen frequencies for these devices remain below 608 MHz. Ideally these should be removed from the cart as they are problematic to use with the house system.

Mobile Analog Mic Case

This system primarily contains analog wireless microphones which should NOT be utilized within the house system due to overlap with identical receivers (SLX4 J3). These should be limited ONLY to use outside of the DHS theatre.

QTY Equipment Notes
1 Shure UA844+SWB Active Antenna Distribution (470-960 MHz)
2 Shure-Compatible UHF Antenna Active Directional Antennas (400-970 MHz)
6 Shure SLX4 (J3 572–596 MHz)2 Analog Receivers for Belt Packs WMIC11-16
1 Tascam CD-200 CD Player

2 Due to limited space within the 572-596 MHz space and overlap from local high-powered TV stations, the use of these receivers is NOT recommended within the DHS theatre. The chance of interference and drop-outs is high and is made worse due to overcrowding with similar SLX4 models in the tech booth.
